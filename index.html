Hello! My name is Neha and this website is a small introduction about me, my interests, and the things I enjoy learning about. I am currently a 3rd year student studying data science and finance, and I enjoy exploring how data can be used to understand the world better. One of my passions is traveling and learning more about the world that I live in. So far, I have visited 20 countries, and I was even part of the Global Scholars where I spent a semester abroad in London. During my time there, since I had no classes on Fridays, I traveled to various countries every weekend. In the end of the 4 months, I went on 32 total flights and explored most of Europe. My favorite country I have visited so far is New Zealand. We flew into Auckland, and drove our way down to Queenstown. On the way we stopped at the Waitomo Glow worm caves, Wellington, seaside beaches with black sand, enjoyed the scenery at Lake Tekapo, and then went to Milford Sound near Queenstown. The beauty of New Zealand cannot be found anywhere else, in all the countries I have explored. The people are kind, the nature is outstanding, and it offered so many exciting adventures.
